‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a QR code?
Open the app, tap "Create QR Code", enter your text or URL, and tap "Generate". Your QR code will be created instantly and can be saved to your library.
Can I scan barcodes from photos?
Yes! Code Pocket App can scan both QR codes and barcodes directly from your camera or from images saved in your photo library.
Where are my saved codes stored?
All your saved codes are stored locally on your device in the app's secure storage. They are not uploaded to any servers and remain private.
What barcode formats are supported?
Code Pocket App supports major barcode formats including UPC, EAN, Code 128, Code 39, and many others, as well as QR codes and Data Matrix codes.
Is the app free to use?
Yes! Code Pocket App is completely free to use with all features included. No in-app purchases or subscriptions required.
